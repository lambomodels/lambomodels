About the Collection

This is the LAMBOMODELS collection — a dedicated catalogue of Lamborghini scale models built over many years. Not a hobby. An empire.

The collection aims to document every Lamborghini model from 1963 to 2024 based on specific criteria — covering every significant production car, concept, one-off, race car, and affiliated brand that has carried the raging bull. The criteria for inclusion will be detailed here in full.

It goes further — tractors, marine engines, concepts that never reached production, one-of-a-kind specials, race cars, motorcycles, and the related cars that share Lamborghini DNA.

Every model is catalogued with its scale, manufacturer, color, year of acquisition, estimated price, and the real car's production year.
